Problem to Product

Created by Mohammad Dawud, last modified on Jun 08, 2021

  1. PROBLEM

    1. WHAT is the problem

    2. Do you have a personal relation with the problem

  2. BRAINSTORM

    1. Talk to your friends and colleagues who can be your potential co-founders

  3. FEASIBILITY-CHECK

    1. Check the possibility to develop the solution

  4. UNIQUENESS

    1. WHAT are your unique skills that will solve this problem

  5. MVP (Minimum Viable Product)

    1. WHAT is the product

    2. (optional) Talk to the potential users before first MVP

    3. TIPS

      1. Release fast

      2. Timebox specs

      3. Write down specs

      4. Only include features that can be completed in the timebox

        1. Very Limited Function: focus only the initial hand picked users and there highest order problems

        2. Change the Initial Product if Needed: Do not fall in love with your initial MVP

        3. Cut the Feature List: to meet the timebox deadline cut whatever needs to be cut, better to have something than nothing by the end of the timebox

      5. Hold problems tightly, Hold customers tightly, Hold solutions loosely.

  6. TESTUSERS (iterate with 5.)

    1. WHO is your first users, pick desperate customers willing to use your shitty MVP regularly

    2. Talk to them for Feedback

  7. PRODUCT / LAUNCH

    1. Day when you get your first customer

    2. Iterate your product after launch based on new customer feedbacks and their needs
